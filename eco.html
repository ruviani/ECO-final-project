The Problem

Over 380 million tons of plastic are produced every year. A large portion ends up in the oceans, harming marine life and entering our food chain through microplastics.

Causes of Plastic Pollution

  • Over-reliance on single-use plastics
  • Lack of efficient waste disposal systems
  • Consumer ignorance and corporate negligence

Solutions

  • Use reusable products like metal straws and tote bags
  • Support policies banning single-use plastics
  • Participate in clean-up campaigns
  • Spread awareness through social media and education
